Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly is the Silver package?

The Silver package uses prompt engineering resources and ChatGPT to create AI-built landing pages tailored to your requirements.

What is included in the Gold package?

The Gold package includes human-assisted website development for a more personalized and professional approach to your landing pages.

How does the Platinum package work?

The Platinum package offers consulting services at $250/hour to help you leverage AI and improve your landing pages' performance and strategy.

How long does it take to complete a project?

The project timeline depends on the package and complexity of the requirements. Silver package projects are typically faster, while Gold and Platinum may take longer due to human involvement and consultations.

Do you offer any revisions or customizations?

Yes, we offer revisions and customizations for all packages to ensure you're satisfied with the final product. The number of revisions and scope of customizations may vary based on the package you choose.

Is there any ongoing support or maintenance?

We provide ongoing support and maintenance for Gold and Platinum packages. For the Silver package, we offer basic support to help you get started with your AI-built landing page, but ongoing maintenance may require additional fees.
